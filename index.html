ABOUT

Dr. Dragana Spica

Dr. Dragana Spica is Top-rated plastic surgeon in Dubai with almost two decades of experience. Dr Dragana Spica is European and UAE board-certified specialist in Plastic, Reconstructive and Aesthetic surgery. In the world of refined beauty and expert care, Dr. Dragana is known for combining unparalleled skill, artistic precision, and a patient-centric approach to achieve life-changing results in both surgical and minimally invasive aesthetic procedures. Dr. Dragana Spica is Medical Director of " Aesthetics by Kings College Hospital London " in Dubai

Dr. Dragana graduated from the University of Belgrade, Serbia, and further advanced her expertise through a Master's degree in Surgical Anatomy and specialized postgraduate training in Aesthetic and Reconstructive Surgery. She completed her residency training at the Department of Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery in Belgrade and gained extensive experience at the prestigious Clinical Centre of Serbia. Today, she is recognized for delivering natural-looking, personalized results through advanced surgical techniques, helping patients achieve their aesthetic goals with confidence and exceptional care.
